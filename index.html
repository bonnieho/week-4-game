CrystalsCollector!

How to play the game:
A random number will be generated at the start of each game.

There are four "crystal" buttons below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total sum.

You win the game by matching your total sum to the randomly generated target number exactly. If your total sum goes over the amount of the target number, you lose the game.

The value of each crystal button will be unknown to you until you select it.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.

Good luck!
